International collaborations

  • Athena – ESA Science Study Team
    The Athena+ X-ray satellite has been recently proposed as a Large mission for the ESA 2028 launch schedule. I am part of the ESA Science Study Team of the Athena mission.
    https://www.the-athena-x-ray-observatory.eu
  • ESA Astronomy Working Group 2018 – 2020
    The Astronomy Working Group (AWG) provides scientific advice mainly to the Space Science Advisory Committee (SSAC). The AWG members are scientists from the ESA Member States with expertise in the specific disciplines covered by the AWG.
    https://www.cosmos.esa.int/web/awg
  • PHAROS Action Chair 2017 – 2021
    PHAROS is an action funded by the European Cooperation in Science and Technology (COST), aimed at studying Neutron Stars from different physical perspectives.
    http://www.cost.eu/COST_Actions/ca/CA16214
  • Square Kilometer Array collaboration since 2017
    Part of the Working Groups on Transients and Pulsars.
    http://www.atnf.csiro.au/SKA/
  • ESA XMM-Newton User Group since 2015
    The XMM-Newton Users' Group advises ESA on all matters relating to the optimisation of the scientific output of the XMM-Newton mission.
    http://xmm.esac.esa.int/external/xmm_user_support/usersgroup/members/index.shtml/
  • GranTeCan Users Committee 2012 – 2014
    The GTC Users Committee (GUC) is an advisory body to the GTC Director.
    http://www.gtc.iac.es/en/pages/gtc/gtc-users-committee-guc.php
  • eXTP collaboration since 2011
    The enhanced X-ray Timing and Polarimetry mission is a Chinese/ESA mission in Phase B.
    http://gri.rm.iasf.cnr.it/LOFToutreach/en.html
  • Fermi-LAT collaboration since 2008
    Affiliated scientist of the NASA Fermi Large Area Gamma-ray Telescope collaboration (Fermi-LAT).
    http://fermi.gsfc.nasa.gov/
